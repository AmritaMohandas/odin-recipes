Ingredients:
  • Flour
  • Brown Sugar
  • Baking Powder
  • Butter
  • Vanilla
  • Salt
  • Milk
  • Chocolate Chips
  • Walnuts
Steps:
  1. Preheat oven to 350
  2. Mix dry ingredients
  3. Melt butter in pan till golden brown
  4. Mix wet ingredients into dry ingredients
  5. Mix in chocolate chips and walnuts
  6. Chill dough for 30 mins
  7. Portion dough onto baking sheet
  8. Bake for 10 mins
