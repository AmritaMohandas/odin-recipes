Zucchini Bread

  • Zucchini
  • Neutral Oil
  • Vanilla
  • Brown Sugar
  • Salt
  • Flour
  • Baking powder
  • Baking soda
  • Cinnamon powder
  • Nutmeg powder
  • Walnuts
  1. Preheat oven to 350
  2. Shred zucchini and pat dry
  3. Sift dry ingredients together
  4. Mix in wet ingredients, except zucchini
  5. Mix in shredded zucchini
  6. Pour into lined loaf pan and top with chopped walnuts
  7. Bake for 40 to 45 mins
